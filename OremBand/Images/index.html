Welcome to Orem High Bands

The Orem High School Band program, directed by Curtis Winters, offers a variety of ensembles including the Wind Symphony, Symphonic Band, Jazz Band, Percussion, and Marching Band. We emphazise musical excellence through challenging repertoire, auditions, and regular performances at festivals and events. Students benefit from travel opportunities, workshops, and team building experiences. Overall, the program fosters strong musicianship, collaboration, and personal growth.
